# Business Tool Lab Service Guide

## Service Scope

This repository is for small, repeatable reporting and spreadsheet workflow jobs. The work is best
suited for CSV exports, Excel-style tables, shop ledgers, order records, inventory sheets, settlement
exports, and follow-up lists.

Good fits:

- Turning one messy table into a readable summary.
- Finding exception rows such as missing settlement, refund review, stock risk, or overdue follow-up.
- Building a small browser-based prototype for a repeated report.
- Writing a monthly digest format with rules and handoff notes.

Bad fits:

- Full accounting systems.
- Private customer databases.
- Medical, legal, or financial advice.
- Anything that requires passwords, account access, or confidential company data.

## Packages

### Starter Cleanup

Clean one messy table and return a readable summary, issue list, and corrected file structure.

### Workflow Prototype

Build a small browser-based tool for one recurring report, checklist, reconciliation, inventory check,
or data cleanup step.

### Monthly Digest Setup

Set up a repeatable monthly report format with sample data, rules, output wording, and handoff notes.

## What I Need From You

- The business type.
- The report or decision you want to improve.
- A fake or anonymized sample with 3-10 rows.
- The output you want: summary, exception list, cleaned table, or repeatable tool.
- Any rules you already use manually.

Do not send private customer data, account numbers, medical information, passwords, internal company
secrets, or live exports from systems you do not have permission to share.
